What is a vinyl wrap?

>

Compared to a case, which adds bulk to your phone, a wrap is a removable vinyl sticker that attaches directly to the back of your device. Our wraps are fully customisable to feature any design you could image, unlike most cases, which are limited to whatever is availible in stores.


Each custom wrap is produced with a unique two-layer design. A layer of printed vinyl displays your chosen image, with a clear coat layer sealing the ink for a durable finish.

How can I purchase a custom wrap?

>

1. Create your wrap using the tool on the custom wraps page.


2. Download the image produced from clicking confirm design.


3. Send an email to rewrapvinyl@gmail.com and attach the downloaded image. Please include name and applicable order details:


   - Wrap or wrap+case bundle.


   - Form class for wrap to be delivered to (if Kingsway school student).


   - Address for wrap to be shipped to (if buying from outside Kingsway, bank details will be sent afterwards).


   - Preferred payment method if picking up in person (bank or cash).



Each custom wrap takes time to produce, so please allow for up to two weeks to receive your product.

What happens if I want to remove the wrap?

>

Unlike normal stickers, vinyl is specially designed to leave no residue when peeled. Rest assured that your glossy glass phone will retain it's finish if you even choose to remove one of our wraps.
