What is a vinyl wrap?

>

Compared to a case, which adds bulk to your phone, a wrap is a removable vinyl sticker that attaches directly to the back of your device. Our wraps are fully customisable to feature any design you could image, unlike most cases, which are limited to whatever is availible in stores.


Each custom wrap is produced with a unique two-layer design. A layer of printed vinyl displays your chosen image, with a clear coat layer sealing the ink for a durable finish.
